Thor's Hammer Steak

Beautiful steak made with a hammer for the hungry

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat oil in the pan at max temp
  2. Add butter to the oil
  3. Add garlic
  4. Sear the steak for 1 min on each side
  5. Add salt and pepper
